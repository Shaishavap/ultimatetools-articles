## Tips for 6 and 7 Letter Wordle

**Start with common letter combinations.** For 6-letter words, opening guesses with words that contain STARNE or SATORI cover the most common English letter frequencies.

**Pay attention to word endings.** Many 6 and 7-letter words end in common suffixes: -ING, -TION, -LY, -ED, -ER, -EST. If your greens cluster at the end, the suffix is probably one of these.

**Use 7-letter mode for vocabulary building.** Less common words appear at 7 letters — and deducing them forces you to think about vocabulary you know but rarely use actively.
