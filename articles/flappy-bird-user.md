## How to Play

**One input. One goal.**

Press **Space**, click anywhere on the game area, or tap on mobile to make the bird flap upward. Release and gravity pulls it back down. Navigate through the gaps between pipes without touching them or the ground.

Each pipe you clear earns **one point**.

## Strategy Tips

**Small frequent flaps, not big jumps.** One big flap sends you too high — you overcorrect and hit the top pipe. Aim for the middle of the gap and make small corrections.

**Look at the next pipe, not the current one.** By the time a pipe is on you, you should already be positioning for it. Train yourself to read one pipe ahead.

**Don't panic at the bottom.** A common mistake is over-flapping when low. One controlled flap from a low position is better than two rapid ones that shoot you into the top pipe.

**The first 5 pipes decide your mindset.** Going slow, starting cautious, and surviving the early pipes builds the rhythm you need for the faster later pipes. Don't rush the opening.
