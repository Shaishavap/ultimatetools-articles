## The lease knows your state

US landlord-tenant law differs by state, so the residential lease adapts to it. Choose your state and the agreement applies that state's statutory **security-deposit limit**, **deposit-return deadline**, and **commonly required disclosures** — plus the federal lead-paint disclosure for homes built before 1978. All 50 states and Washington, D.C. are covered, so a California lease and a Texas lease come out with the right deposit rules instead of one-size-fits-all boilerplate.
