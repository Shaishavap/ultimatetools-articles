## How to Play

## Tips

**Watch the whole grid, not one mole.** The biggest score jump comes when you stop reacting to individual moles and start scanning all 9 holes simultaneously. Your peripheral vision does the work.

**Click the moment you see movement.** Don't wait for the mole to fully rise — click as soon as any mole starts appearing. The 700ms window is short.

**Don't miss the edges.** Corner and edge holes get ignored when players focus on the center. Train yourself to check all 9 holes equally.

**The final 15 seconds is where scores are made.** Even a distracted first 45 seconds leaves 15 seconds of fast spawning. Stay sharp for the finish.
