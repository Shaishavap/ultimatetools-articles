## The standard block format

Block format is the professional default. Everything is left-aligned, with a blank line between blocks:

1. Your name and contact details
2. The date
3. The recipient's name, title, company, and address
4. A subject line ("Re: …")
5. The salutation ("Dear …,")
6. The body
7. A closing ("Sincerely,") and your name

The generator places every block correctly, so you only write the message.

## How to write the body

Lead with the purpose in the first line, use the body for the detail or request, and close with a clear next step. A subject line tells the reader the topic at a glance — always include one.
