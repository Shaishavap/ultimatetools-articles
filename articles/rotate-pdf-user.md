# How to Rotate a PDF Online for Free — Rotate One Page or All Pages, No Software Needed

A scanned document came out sideways. A PDF report has one landscape page in the middle of portrait pages. You need to fix the orientation — without installing software or paying for Acrobat.

The [Rotate PDF](https://ultimatetools.io/tools/pdf-tools/rotate-pdf/) tool at Ultimate Tools fixes page orientation in your browser, with no upload and no account required.

---

## How to Rotate a PDF

1. Open the [Rotate PDF](https://ultimatetools.io/tools/pdf-tools/rotate-pdf/) tool
2. Drop your PDF onto the upload area
3. See all pages as thumbnails
4. Select which pages to rotate (or select all)
5. Choose the rotation: 90° clockwise, 90° counter-clockwise, or 180°
6. Click **Rotate** and download

You can rotate every page at once or just individual pages.

---

## Rotation Options

| Option | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **90° Clockwise** | Rotates right — landscape to portrait (standard fix for sideways scans) |
| **90° Counter-Clockwise** | Rotates left |
| **180°** | Flips upside-down — useful for double-sided scans |

---

## Rotating Individual Pages vs. All Pages

**Rotate all pages:** Select all thumbnails and apply the rotation. Useful when the entire document was scanned in the wrong orientation.

**Rotate specific pages:** Click to select only the pages that are sideways. Leave the correctly-oriented pages untouched. This is common with scanned mixed-orientation documents.

---

## Common Use Cases

**Scanned document is sideways:** The most common case. Every page came through rotated 90°. Select all, rotate 90° clockwise, done.

**One landscape page in a portrait document:** A spreadsheet or diagram was inserted sideways. Select that page only, rotate it, leave the rest alone.

**Double-sided scan, one side upside-down:** Physical pages scanned in a batch often flip every other page 180°. Select alternating pages, rotate 180°.

**Received a rotated PDF:** A client or colleague sent a PDF where pages are hard to read. Fix the orientation before printing or annotating.

**Phone camera scan:** Mobile scanning apps sometimes get the orientation wrong. Correct it before filing or sharing.

---

## Privacy: Runs in Your Browser

The rotation is applied using pdf-lib entirely in your browser. Your PDF is never sent to a server — not even for processing. The file is read locally, rotated in memory, and downloaded back to your device.

This matters for:
- Legal or medical documents
- Financial PDFs
- Any file you wouldn't want on a third-party server
