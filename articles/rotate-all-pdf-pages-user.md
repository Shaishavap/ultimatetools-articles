## How to Rotate All PDF Pages at Once

1. Go to the [Rotate PDF tool](https://ultimatetools.io/tools/pdf-tools/rotate-pdf/)
2. Click **Choose File** and select your PDF
3. All pages load as thumbnails
4. Click **Select All** to select every page
5. Choose your rotation — **90° clockwise**, **90° counter-clockwise**, or **180°**
6. Click **Apply Rotation**
7. Download the corrected PDF

Every page rotates in one action. No clicking through pages individually.

## Can You Rotate Only Some Pages?

Yes. Instead of selecting all, click individual page thumbnails to select only the pages you want to rotate. You can mix rotations — rotate some pages 90° and others 180° — before applying.

This is useful for:
- Scanned documents where one section was fed in upside down
- PDFs combining portrait and landscape pages that need reorientation
- Reports where an appendix page is rotated differently from the body

## Why PDFs End Up Rotated Wrong

**Scanners** — Flatbed and sheet-fed scanners sometimes default to landscape output. If documents are placed face-down or at the wrong orientation, every page comes out rotated.

**Mobile scanning apps** — Phone camera scanning apps occasionally misread page orientation, especially for A4 pages scanned in portrait mode.

**PDF merging** — When combining PDFs from different sources, some pages may have been created in landscape while others are portrait. Merging preserves each page's original rotation.

**Printer settings** — PDFs generated from print dialogs can inherit the wrong page orientation if the printer driver settings don't match the document layout.

---

## Rotation Angles Explained

**90° clockwise** — Turns each page a quarter turn to the right. Use when content is rotated 90° counter-clockwise (lying on its left side).

**90° counter-clockwise** — Turns each page a quarter turn to the left. Use when content is rotated 90° clockwise (lying on its right side).

**180°** — Flips each page upside down. Use when content is completely inverted.

If you're unsure, apply the rotation and check the preview before downloading.
