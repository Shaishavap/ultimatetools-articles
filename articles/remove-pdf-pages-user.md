## Step-by-Step

**Step 1 — Open the tool**

Go to [Remove PDF Pages](https://ultimatetools.io/tools/pdf-tools/remove-pdf-pages/).

## Common Use Cases

**Removing blank pages**
Scanners often produce blank pages when scanning double-sided documents. Select all blank page thumbnails and delete them in one pass.

**Stripping a cover page**
Many PDFs arrive with a title/cover page you don't need for internal distribution. Select it and remove it.

**Deleting confidential sections**
Need to share part of a document but not all of it? Remove the pages you don't want to share before sending.

**Trimming a report**
A 40-page report has 10 pages of appendix data you don't need. Select those pages, delete, download the trimmed version.

**Fixing scanned documents**
Accidentally scanned the same page twice? Remove the duplicate.

## Notes on Page Order

Removing pages does not reorder the remaining pages — the page numbers shift down, but the content order stays exactly as-is. If you need to rearrange pages as well, use a PDF editor after removing the unwanted pages.

## Privacy

Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No file is uploaded to any server. The modified PDF is generated locally and downloaded directly to your device.
