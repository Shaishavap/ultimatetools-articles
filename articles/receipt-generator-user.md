## A receipt is not an invoice

This trips people up constantly:

- An **invoice** is a *request* for payment — it goes out before the money arrives.
- A **receipt** is *confirmation* that payment was received — it goes out after.

If you send an "invoice" once you've already been paid, it looks like you're billing twice. Use the [free invoice generator with tax and PDF download](https://ultimatetools.io/tools/misc-tools/invoice-generator/) before payment, and this receipt generator after.
