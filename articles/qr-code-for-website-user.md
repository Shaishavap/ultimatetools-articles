## What URL to Use

**Homepage:** `https://yoursite.com/`
Point people to your main site from a business card, product packaging, or a sign.

**Specific landing page:** `https://yoursite.com/contact/`
Send people directly to a contact form, booking page, or product page — skip the homepage navigation entirely.

**Social profile:** `https://instagram.com/yourbrand`
Point customers from a physical location to your social media without them having to search.

**Google Maps location:** `https://maps.google.com/...`
Add to your storefront or menu. Customers tap → directions open instantly.

**Portfolio or resume:** `https://yourname.com/`
Print on the bottom of a physical resume or business card.

## PNG vs SVG — Which Format to Download?

**PNG** — Use for digital: email signatures, website embeds, social media posts, presentations.

**SVG** — Use for print: business cards, banners, flyers, merchandise. SVG scales to any size without pixelating. A business card printer will always prefer SVG.

## Will the QR Code Ever Stop Working?

Static QR codes generated here are permanent. The code encodes the URL directly — there is no intermediate redirect or server involved. As long as your website URL stays live, the QR code works forever.
