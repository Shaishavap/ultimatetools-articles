Link a QR code to a product page, manual, or how-to video and print it on packaging.

## PNG vs SVG

**PNG** — Use for digital sharing: WhatsApp, email, social media, websites. Fixed resolution — looks best at 1000px or above.

**SVG** — Use for print: business cards, flyers, posters, banners. Scales to any size without pixelation.
