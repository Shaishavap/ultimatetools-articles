## Is an Electronic Signature Legally Valid?

For most business documents: yes. Electronic signatures are legally recognized under:

- **US** — the ESIGN Act and UETA
- **EU** — eIDAS Regulation (as a Simple Electronic Signature)
- **UK** — Electronic Communications Act 2000
- **India** — Information Technology Act, 2000

Exceptions include wills, court orders, real estate deeds, and notarized documents in most jurisdictions — those still require wet ink or a qualified digital certificate.
