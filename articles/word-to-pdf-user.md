## Why Convert to PDF?

**Locking the layout:** PDF preserves exact formatting across every device and operating system. What you see is what the recipient sees — fonts, spacing, and layout stay fixed regardless of what they use to open it.

**Compatibility:** Not everyone has Microsoft Word. A PDF opens natively in every browser, on every platform, without any software.

**Submission portals:** Job applications, government forms, academic submissions — most require PDF. Even if you wrote the document in Word, the portal wants a PDF.

**Preventing edits:** Once converted to PDF, the content can't be accidentally modified during review.

## Privacy: No Upload Required

The conversion runs server-side using Mammoth (for DOCX parsing) and PDFKit, but the file is processed in your session only — it is not stored, indexed, or retained after the response is delivered.

For documents containing sensitive information (contracts, personal statements, legal documents), this is an important distinction from cloud storage tools that save your files to their servers permanently.
