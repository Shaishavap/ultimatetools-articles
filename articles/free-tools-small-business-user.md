# 9 Free Browser Tools for Small Business Owners — No Monthly Subscriptions

Small business owners manage documents, client communications, invoices, and security across multiple platforms — often without a dedicated IT team. The default answer for most of these tasks is a monthly subscription to Adobe, FreshBooks, DocuSign, or similar. But many of the most common tasks don't require that kind of spend.

These free tools from [Ultimate Tools](https://ultimatetools.io/) cover the most frequent small business document and utility needs in the browser — no downloads, no monthly fees, no file upload to a third-party server.

## 1. Invoice Generator — Professional invoices without FreshBooks

The [Invoice Generator](https://ultimatetools.io/tools/misc-tools/invoice-generator/) creates PDF invoices with your business name, client details, line items, tax rate, and payment terms. The PDF generates in your browser and downloads directly — no subscription, no limits on how many you create.

**Use case:** Monthly invoices to recurring clients with itemized services and tax.

The [PDF Merger](https://ultimatetools.io/tools/pdf-tools/merge-pdf/) combines multiple PDF files into one — useful for bundling a contract with a scope of work, or attaching supporting documents to a proposal. Files never leave your device.

The [PDF eSign tool](https://ultimatetools.io/tools/pdf-tools/sign-pdf/) adds a signature to any PDF directly in the browser. Draw your signature, type it, or upload an image. For straightforward business agreements where a full DocuSign workflow isn't necessary, this covers the job.

**Use case:** Signing a vendor agreement or freelance contract sent by a client.
