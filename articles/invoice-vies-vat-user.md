## Validate an EU VAT number live (VIES)

This is the feature most free tools don't have. For B2B sales inside the EU you often apply the **reverse charge** — but only if the client's VAT number is genuinely registered. Get it wrong and the VAT is on you.

In EU mode, enter the client's VAT number with its country code (e.g. `DE123456789`) and click **Validate (VIES)**. The tool checks it live against the EU's **official VIES database** and shows whether it's valid plus the registered business name:

> IE6388047V → ✓ Valid — GOOGLE IRELAND LIMITED

No more copy-pasting into the clunky VIES website. (Note: UK numbers left VIES after Brexit — verify those via HMRC.)

## US sales tax by state, auto-filled

The US has no federal sales tax and the rate depends on where you are. Select **USA**, pick your state, and the **base statewide rate fills in automatically** — California 7.25%, Texas 6.25%, and so on. It's a base rate (city/county rates can add more), and it stays editable, so you start from the right number instead of guessing.

Client quoted you a gross, VAT-inclusive figure? The built-in **Reverse VAT calculator** (UK/EU) takes a gross amount and rate and shows the net + VAT breakdown — handy when a price already includes VAT and you need it itemised.
