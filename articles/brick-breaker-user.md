## How to Play Brick Breaker

**Goal:** Use the paddle to bounce a ball and break all bricks on the board. Clear every brick to win. Lose all lives and it's game over.

**Controls:**

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| Move paddle | Mouse movement or ← → arrow keys |
| Launch ball | Click or press Space to start |
| Pause | Press Escape |

---

## Rules

- **Lives:** You start with 3 lives. If the ball falls below the paddle, you lose one life. Lose all 3 and the game ends.
- **Breaking bricks:** The ball bounces off bricks on contact and destroys them. Each brick destroyed adds to your score.
- **Ball angle:** The angle the ball rebounds at depends on where it hits the paddle — hit it off the left edge to send it left, off the right edge to send it right. Use this to aim.
- **Level progression:** Clearing all bricks advances you to the next level with a fresh brick layout.

---

## Tips

**Keep the ball out of corners.** A ball trapped in a corner bouncing back and forth on the top wall will clear the remaining bricks — but if it drops, it's hard to recover. Stay focused.

**Use paddle edges intentionally.** Center hits bounce the ball back roughly where it came from. Edge hits sharply redirect it. Use edge hits to target specific sections of the brick grid.

**Clear bottom rows first.** Bricks at the bottom are harder to reach after the upper bricks are gone. Clear a path downward early so the ball can reach isolated bottom bricks.

**Don't chase — anticipate.** Watch the ball's angle after every bounce to predict where it's heading. Move the paddle to where it will be, not where it is.
