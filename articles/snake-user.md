## How to Play Snake

**Goal:** Eat food to grow the snake as long as possible. Don't run into the walls or yourself.

**Controls:**

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| Arrow keys | Change direction |
| W / A / S / D | Alternative directional controls |
| Space | Pause / Resume |
| Enter | Restart after game over |

The snake moves continuously in its current direction. You steer — you don't stop.

---

## Rules

- **Eat food** — the food dot appears randomly on the grid. Move the snake's head onto it to eat. The snake grows one segment.
- **Avoid walls** — the snake dies if its head hits the edge of the grid.
- **Avoid yourself** — if the snake's head hits its own body, game over.
- **Score** — each food eaten adds to your score. The higher the score, the more the snake has grown.

---

## Tips for a High Score

**Plan ahead.** At short lengths it's easy to steer freely. At long lengths, your previous path blocks new routes. Think a few moves ahead about where you'll be after eating the next piece of food.

**Spiral inward.** One reliable strategy: hug the walls in a large coil and spiral inward, keeping the body organized rather than crossing randomly.

**Don't panic near walls.** Most deaths happen when a player overcorrects near an edge. Commit to your direction and adjust early, not at the last moment.

**180° reversal kills you.** You cannot reverse directly — the snake can't turn back into itself. Left → Right and Up → Down are instant deaths.
