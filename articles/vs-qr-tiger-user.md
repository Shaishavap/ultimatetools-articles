## What QR Tiger offers

QR Tiger covers both static and dynamic QR codes. Dynamic QR codes let you change the destination URL after printing — useful for campaigns. It supports custom colors, logos embedded in the center, and frame designs. There's also analytics tracking for dynamic codes.

The free plan allows a limited number of dynamic QR codes, adds a watermark to downloads, and requires account creation for most features.

## The watermark problem

QR Tiger's free tier downloads include a QR Tiger watermark. This is fine for personal testing, but it makes the QR code unusable for any professional context — print materials, packaging, signage, business cards.

[Ultimate Tools QR generator](https://ultimatetools.io/tools/misc-tools/bulk-qr-code-generator/) has no watermark on any download, free or otherwise. There's no account concept at all — you open the page and generate.

## Where QR Tiger wins

**Dynamic QR codes** — If you need to change the URL after printing (e.g., you've printed 1,000 flyers and need to redirect to a new page), dynamic QR codes are the right solution. QR Tiger supports these; Ultimate Tools does not. This is a genuine use case for agencies and large campaigns.

**Logo embedding** — QR Tiger can embed a logo or brand image in the center of the QR code. Useful for branded campaigns where the QR code is part of a design system.

**Analytics** — QR Tiger tracks scans by location, device, and time. Useful if you need to know which QR code placement is performing.

---

## Where Ultimate Tools wins

**Free bulk generation** — QR Tiger's bulk and CSV features require a paid plan. Ultimate Tools generates up to 500 QR codes from a CSV file for free.

**No watermark** — Every QR code downloads clean. No paid upgrade required for print-ready output.

**No account** — No signup form, no email verification, no dashboard to manage. Open the page, generate, download.

**Privacy** — QR codes are generated in your browser. The URLs or data you encode never leave your device. QR Tiger sends your data to their servers for processing and analytics.

**No file size or scan limits** — The free QR Tiger plan limits the number of dynamic QR codes. Ultimate Tools has no limits on static QR generation.
